## What to review

The good version exposes navigation, main content, article content, and a real heading through native HTML.

The bad version may look acceptable, but it hides navigation and content meaning behind generic containers.

## Review notes

In review, ask whether another developer or assistive technology can understand the page outline without reading CSS. Semantic HTML is not decoration; it is part of the interface contract.
