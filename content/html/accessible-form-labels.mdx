## What to review

The good version gives the email field a persistent accessible name by pairing the `label` and `input` with matching identifiers.

The bad version depends on placeholder text, which disappears as soon as someone types and does not provide the same durable relationship as a label.

## Review notes

In review, check whether every control can be identified without relying on visual position or temporary hint text. Placeholder-only fields can render neatly, but they make the form harder to review, translate, autofill, and use with assistive technology.
