## What to review

The good version creates a predictable outline: page title, section, then subsection.

The bad version jumps between heading levels because the markup is trying to control appearance instead of structure.

## Review notes

Ask whether a reader can skim only headings and understand the page. If the answer is no, the heading hierarchy is probably carrying style decisions instead of information architecture.
